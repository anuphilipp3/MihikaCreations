Our Plan for Your Website

A simple note to explain what we're thinking — and to check we're on the right track before we start.

First, what we understand about Mahika

We spent time going through your Instagram. Please tell us if we've got it right.

  • You make beautiful designer dresses for women, in lovely fabrics like Chanderi, cotton, linen, muslin and organza — with hand embroidery and thread work.
  • Your biggest strength: you make dresses to fit each customer — custom size, custom colour. Most shops don't do this.
  • Customers buy for real moments — Easter & church, baptisms, festivals, weddings, office, and everyday wear.
  • Today everything happens over Instagram DM and WhatsApp — customers message to ask the price, and you reply one by one.
  • You deliver across Kerala, and you've just opened your shop — congratulations! 🎉

What's slowing things down today

Nothing is broken — your business works. But a few things make it harder than it needs to be:

  1. Customers can't see prices. Everyone must message you just to ask. That's a lot of replying — and some give up before asking.
  2. There's no proper place to see all your dresses. On Instagram, older dresses get buried and can't be searched by occasion, colour or fabric.
  3. You can only sell in Kerala. Malayali women across India would love your dresses but can't reach you.
  4. Mahika has no "home" online yet — a place that looks premium and feels like your brand.

What we want to build for you

A website of your own — think of it as your shop, open 24 hours, that anyone can visit.

  • All your dresses in one beautiful place, with clear prices.
  • Customers browse easily — by occasion, colour, fabric, size.
  • They pick their custom size and choices right on the website.
  • Then they finish the order on your WhatsApp — just like now. The website does the hard part; the order lands in your WhatsApp neatly.

You keep the part that works — the personal WhatsApp conversation — and the website removes the tiring part: answering "what's the price?" and "is it available?" a hundred times.

We'll protect what makes Mahika special

Your made-to-measure service is your heart, so the website is built around it:

  • The customer gives her key measurements on the site (a simple "how to measure" guide helps her get it right at home).
  • She picks her colour and any changes she wants.
  • The fine details — embroidery, final fit, delivery date — you still confirm personally on WhatsApp.
  • Since each dress is made specially for her, it can't be returned — but we offer one free fit adjustment, so she feels safe ordering.

The look and feel we're imagining

Soft, elegant, and premium — like your dresses.

  • Warm, gentle colours taken from your own outfits — creams, soft pinks, greens.
  • Beautiful close-up photos of the fabric and hand work, so people can almost feel the quality.
  • Calm and graceful — not loud or cluttered. Like walking into a lovely boutique.

We'd also tidy the brand name. Today it shows as both "Mahika Creations" and "Mahika Designs." We suggest simply Mahika — clean, premium, easy to remember.

How we'll roll it out — one step at a time

We won't do everything at once. We'll build in calm, sensible steps:

  1. Get the look right — name, logo, colours, and good photos.
  2. Build the dress collection page with prices — the heart of the site.
  3. Add the made-to-measure (custom size) feature.
  4. Connect it to your WhatsApp so orders arrive cleanly.
  5. Go live and point your Instagram to the website.
  6. Open up to all of India — to sell beyond Kerala.

We start with Kerala (what you do today) and grow to all of India once everything runs smoothly.

A few things we'd love you to confirm

  1. Are we understanding your business correctly? (The dresses, the customers, the custom-fit service.)
  2. Showing prices openly — are you comfortable? This is the single biggest thing that saves your time and brings more orders. (Special/bridal pieces can stay "price on request.")
  3. The name "Mahika" — do you like it as the main brand name?
  4. Photos — do you have good clear photos, or should we plan a small shoot?
  5. Selling across India later — is that a direction you want to grow towards?

We want to give Mahika its own beautiful online home — where customers can see your
dresses and prices, choose their custom fit, and order through your WhatsApp —
so you sell more, with less effort, to more people.

Does this feel right to you? Tell us what you love, what you'd change, and anything we've misunderstood.
