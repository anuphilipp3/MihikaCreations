# Mahika — Our Plan for Your Website

*A simple note to explain what we're thinking, and to check we're on the right track before we start.*

---

## First, here's what we understand about Mahika

We spent time going through your Instagram, and here's what we see — please tell us if we've got it right:

- You make **beautiful designer dresses for women**, stitched in lovely fabrics like Chanderi, cotton, linen, muslin and organza, with **hand embroidery and thread work**.
- Your biggest strength is that you **make dresses to fit each customer** — custom size, custom colour. That's special. Most shops don't do this.
- Your customers buy for **real moments** — Easter and church, baptisms, festivals, weddings, office, and everyday wear.
- Right now, everything happens through **Instagram DM and WhatsApp**. Customers see a dress, message you, and ask "what's the price?" — and then you reply to each one, one by one.
- You deliver **across Kerala**, and you've just **opened your shop** — congratulations! 🎉

If any of this is wrong, just tell us. We want to understand your business properly before building anything.

---

## What's slowing things down today

Nothing is broken — your business works. But a few things make it harder than it needs to be:

1. **Customers can't see prices.** Every single person has to message you just to ask the price. That's a lot of replying for you, and some customers give up before asking.
2. **There's no proper place to see all your dresses.** On Instagram, old dresses get buried. A customer can't easily look through everything or search by occasion, colour, or fabric.
3. **You can only sell in Kerala right now.** There are Malayali women all over India who would love your dresses but can't reach you.
4. **Mahika doesn't yet have its own "home" online** — a place that looks premium and feels like *your* brand, not just an Instagram page.

---

## What we want to build for you

A **website of your own** — think of it as your shop, open 24 hours, that anyone can visit.

Here is how it will work, in simple terms:

- **All your dresses in one beautiful place**, with **clear prices**, so customers know exactly what they're looking at.
- Customers can **look through everything easily** — by occasion (church, festival, wedding), by colour, by fabric, by size.
- When a customer likes a dress and wants it made for her size, she fills in her **measurements and choices right on the website**.
- Then — and this is important — she **finishes the order on WhatsApp, just like now.** The website does the hard part (showing the dress, the price, her size, her choices), and it all lands in your WhatsApp neatly. **You don't lose your personal touch.** You still talk to your customer.

So you keep the part that works (the personal WhatsApp conversation), and the website removes the part that's tiring (answering "what's the price?" and "is it available?" a hundred times).

---

## We'll protect what makes Mahika special

Your made-to-measure service is your heart, so the website is built around it:

- The customer gives her **key measurements** on the site (a simple "how to measure" guide will help her do it right at home).
- She picks her **colour and any changes** she wants.
- The fine details (embroidery, final fit confirmation, delivery date) you still confirm with her **personally on WhatsApp** — the way you do now.
- Since these dresses are made specially for her, they **can't be returned** — but we'll offer **one free fit adjustment**, so she feels safe ordering.

---

## The look and feel we're imagining

Soft, elegant, and premium — like your dresses.

- Warm, gentle colours taken straight from your own outfits (creams, soft pinks, greens).
- Beautiful close-up photos of the **fabric and the hand work**, so people can almost feel the quality.
- Calm and graceful, **not loud or cluttered**. When someone visits, it should feel like walking into a lovely boutique.

We'll also tidy up the brand name. Right now it shows as both "Mahika Creations" and "Mahika Designs." We suggest simply **Mahika** — clean, premium, and easy to remember.

---

## How we'll roll it out (one step at a time)

We won't do everything at once. We'll build it in calm, sensible steps:

1. **Get the look right** — name, logo, colours, and good photos.
2. **Build the dress collection page** with prices — the heart of the site.
3. **Add the made-to-measure (custom size) feature.**
4. **Connect it to your WhatsApp** so orders come to you cleanly.
5. **Go live**, point your Instagram to the website, and welcome customers.
6. **Then open up to all of India** — so you can sell beyond Kerala.

We start with **Kerala** (what you do today) and grow to **all of India** once everything is running smoothly.
